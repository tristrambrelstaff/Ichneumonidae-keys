Face smooth and shining. Fore wing more than 5mm long. Spiracle of propodeum with rim pale. Male and sometimes female with scutellum yellow-marked laterally.
TAXON= Bioblapsis polita (Vollenhoven)
-
Face coriaceous and matt. Fore wing less than 5mm long. Spiracle of propodeum with rim dark. Scutellum entirely black in both sexes.
TAXON= Bioblapsis cultiformis (Davis)
